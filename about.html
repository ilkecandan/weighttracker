Weight Tracker

What is Weight Tracker?

Weight Tracker is a simple mobile app that helps you keep track of your weight. It works offline, stores everything on your device, and gives you a clear view of your progress. No clutter and no extra steps, just you and your numbers.

Try the App

Privacy First

We do not collect any of your information. There are no accounts, no trackers, no notifications. All data is stored only on your device using local storage. Your progress belongs to you and only you.

Install as an App

You can install Weight Tracker as a Progressive Web App (PWA) to make it feel just like a regular app:

  • Android (Chrome/Edge): Open the menu and choose “Add to Home Screen”
  • iPhone/iPad (Safari): Tap the share button and select “Add to Home Screen”
  • Desktop (Chrome/Edge): Click the install icon in the address bar
